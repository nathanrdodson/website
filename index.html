Music for Listening


These are some of the songs I have been listening to most recently. Check out my playlist for 2020!

I Must Cry Out Loud by Mother Mother can really only be described as a warranting song for those who need a push to reach into their heart and soul. It's a song that tickles the itch to cry and scream, but at the end of it all you are set free.

Lent by Autoheart is beautifully up-tempo, and is a quirky song that I literally have had on repeat for a while now.

I'm Coming Clean by Ezra Furman is really an awesome song, and I mostly have been listening to it for its relation to the Netflix Original Sex Education that I recently fell in love with...Ezra has a ton of songs that all are quite good, and all are in that show.

HAIRCARE's Blinding Cordoned States is a fantasic piece of Austrailian psychedlic rock that I found through another band. Unfortunately they haven't been around much so they only have three songs. Go give them some <3

Lastly, Quiet Little Voices is an anthem about exploration of the soul. It is angry, confused, and lamenting at times. Fantastic song overall.
